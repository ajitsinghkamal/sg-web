Designer by Profession, Calligraphist by Soul. Precisely Speaking, An Artist as a Whole.

Worked as a Visualizer in Ad Agencies for few years along with freelancing in Design & Calligraphy, I am currently focusing on experimenting & exploring in advanced Typography, Hand Lettering and Calligraphy; parallelly working on Visual & Graphic Design assignments as well.

Graduated from Rachana Sansad College of Applied Art and Craft in the year 2014, today I am associated as a Freelance Typographer at Ogilvy & Mather, Mumbai & DDB Mudra Group, Mumbai. Having worked with Brands and Clients like Big Bazaar, ASK Wealth Management, DMA Awards, Shardul Amarchand Mangaldas & Co., Bank of America, IndusInd Bank, Standard Chartered Bank Bayer, Cambridge University Press, VIP Industries Pvt. Ltd, Safforn Art Gallery, in the recent past, fortunately I also got an opportunity of collaborating with DRAFT FCB-ULKA to pitch for rebranding of “Let Girls Learn”, an initiative by Hon. Michelle Obama & Hon. Barack Obama. A dream came true when I won the competition organized by Govt. of India of designing a Logo for BRICS Summit 2016 and was Honoured in the hands of External Affairs Minister of India, Smt. Sushma Swaraj for the same.

My Inspiration isn’t one or two but a diversified group of Artists, Calligraphers, Typographers, Thinkers, Writers, Poets, Actors, Innovators, Creative Directors, to name a few. My Objective in life is to learn and practice all the Indian scripts and the languages followed by foreign languages. In a long run, looking forward to have my own Design Studio specializing in Typography, Lettering & Calligraphy, ultimately making people smile with whatever I do.
